About Me

I'm Khad — a photographer and visual storyteller. My lens finds its home in the raw energy of sports, the quiet poetry of city streets, and the unguarded truth of human portraits.

Every frame is a decision. Every shot, a conversation between light, time, and intention. I don't just capture moments — I find the ones worth keeping.
